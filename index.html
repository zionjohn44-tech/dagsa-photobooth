WELCOME

Welcome to DAGSA 2026!

DAGSA is DWOL's (Deep and Wide One Love) annual gathering that brings together every family group under one roof for a night of worship, fellowship, and celebration. This year's theme is "One Love" — a reminder that no matter which FAM you belong to, we are one body, one heart, one family in Christ.

Join us as we celebrate togetherness, honor God through praise, and make memories that will last a lifetime. Welcome home, DAGSA family!

Preaching Summary

Tonight's Message

"One Love, One Body"

📖 1 Corinthians 12:12–13

In a world that constantly pulls us apart, God calls us to something greater — unity. Tonight's message reminds us that just as the body has many parts but remains one, so too are we: different FAMs, different backgrounds, different stories — but one Spirit, one baptism, one love.

As we gather tonight, let this truth anchor us: You are not alone. You belong here. You are loved. May every song sung, every prayer prayed, and every laugh shared remind us that we are deeply and widely loved by our Father.

01God's love unites what the world divides.
02Every FAM is essential — no one is extra.
03Our strength is found in our togetherness.

ABOUT US

What is DAGSA?

DAGSA — short for "Dagsa" (meaning "surge" or "overflow") — is DWOL's flagship annual gathering for all FAMs under the ICC Youth Bayambang umbrella. It's a night where every generation of young believers comes together to worship, celebrate, and be reminded of what matters most: love — God's love, and ours for one another.

This website was created to document the beauty of DAGSA 2026 — from the powerful word shared, to the four FAMs that make up our community, to the photobooth memories you'll treasure for years to come.

Whether you're a member finding your FAM photos, a first-timer getting to know us, or a parent checking in on your kids — welcome! You're part of this story too. 💛
