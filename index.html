"Next Level, Same God"

📖 Hebrews 13:8 — "Jesus Christ is the same yesterday, today, and forever."

Life is full of transitions — and with every transition comes a mix of emotions: excitement, nervousness, and even fear. But no matter what season we enter, God has a promise: He goes with us.

Just as God worked through Joseph, David, and Joshua, He is also working in us today. What may seem difficult now may actually be God's preparation for something greater tomorrow. As Genesis 50:20 says, "God intended it for good."

ABOUT US

What is DAGSA?

DAGSA — short for "Dagsa" (meaning "surge" or "overflow") — is DWOL's flagship annual gathering for all FAMs under the ICC Youth Bayambang umbrella. It's a night where every generation of young believers comes together to worship, celebrate, and be reminded of what matters most: love — God's love, and ours for one another.

This website was created to document the beauty of DAGSA 2026 — from the powerful word shared, to the four FAMs that make up our community, to the photobooth memories you'll treasure for years to come.

Whether you're a member finding your FAM photos, a first-timer getting to know us, or a parent checking in on your kids — welcome! You're part of this story too. 💛
